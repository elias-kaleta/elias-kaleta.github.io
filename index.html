About Me

I'm a digital enthusiast who has recently graduated with a degree in Creative Technology from AUT. Through my university work and recent internship, I have found that I love working across a range of areas within Digital, and I am able to easily adapt to any team or situation. I'm half Samoan, half Greek. Outside of work, I have a really broad range of interests and love trying new things! A common question is what does a Bachelor of Creative Technology entail? The answer is not simple, because it encompasses a key trait that also describes my skillset, ambiguity. Both myself and the degree have a multi-disciplinary scope - Product design (Digital and Physical), entrepreneurship, coding and just creating in general. Throughout my professional and academic career, I have gained a vast range of skills. My 7 years in retail taught me communication and people skills; my 3 years studying Creative Technology taught me product design and fabrication, UX/UI design, market research, and how to work in a team-based project; my Creative Media certificate solidified my knowledge of design and the use of most Adobe Suite programs. If you're looking for an individual with a multi-faceted skillset and the ability to adapt to whatever is thrown at them, look no further.
